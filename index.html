About CMS College

CMS Educational and Charitable Trust, established in 1988, is committed to providing quality higher education. The college is accredited with NAAC A++ Grade and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Science, Commerce, Arts and Management.

With modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, digital classrooms, research facilities and strong industry partnerships, CMS ensures holistic development of students.
